So, who am I?

A little word about myself.

Biography

Well, it’s a little unusual to talk about myself, being honest, but I think that everyone need an explanation who are they listening to…

Professional occupation

For the current moment, I work as a reverse engineer on a full-time job.

With overall experience across several companies for about 4 years, I had been worked with a large amount of stuff:

  1. Various NGFWs - Cisco, Sonicwall, Fortinet, Ivanti, etc.
  2. Several smart home networks like Xiaomi & Tuya
  3. A whole bunch of hardware and software solutions, for which I have my very own private knowledge database.

And much, much more…

Outside of being a work horse

Pretty funny, but when I’m not occupied by my work tasks, I do pretty the same - reversing, breaking, tearing and exploring things around me.

I guess somebody can call me a no-life nerd, and, maybe, they’re actually right… for a little bit.

The reason I do it is pretty simple - I love what I do. This is the only point I continue to do what I do, even when life hits hard.

Why this blog exists

The first reason is simple - I just want to post my articles and researches (and maybe some of the translations of my articles on the other platforms) in some place where I don’t have to explain why I decided to put this online.

The second reason… Well, I made a theme for this website by myself, so it would be a pity to let it collect dust on the shelf. A little bit stupid, right?

Anyway, this is my little heaven now. Hope you enjoy reading the stuff I post here.
